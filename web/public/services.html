Commands

Music
/play

Alias: p

Play a song via a search query, Spotify, Youtube, or SoundCloud link.

/pause

Alias: None

Pause the Currently playing track (The bot will disconnect after a while of no resume).
/Resume

Alias: continue

Resume a recently paused track in the queue.
/disconnect

Alias: stop, leave

Wocks application will leave your voice channel, your wock session will be ended.
/skip

Alias: s

Skip to the next track awaiting to be played in the queue.
/repeat

Alias: restart

Restart the currently playing song from your track.
/Volume

Alias: v

Adjust the volume of the Wock player in your voice channel.
/shuffle

Alias: random

Shuffle all songs you have to set play from your queue.
/queue view

Alias: None

View all songs listed to be played in your queue.
/queue clear

Alias: None

Clear all songs that were set to be played in your queue.
/queue remove

Alias: None

Remove a song previously set to be played on your track for your queue.
/queue move

Alias: None

Move the position of a song in your track from your queue, from one position to another.
/Preset vaporwave

Alias: None

Timescale preset which slows down the currently playing track, matching up to vaporwave, a slowed-down genre of music.
/Preset nightcore

Alias: None

Timescale preset which speeds up the currently playing track, matching up to nightcore, a genre of sped-up music.
/preset remove

Alias: None

Remove the applied preset from your track
voicemaster
/voicemaster setup

Alias: vm setup

Setup the voicemaster configuration for your guild.
/Voicemaster reset

Alias: vm reset

Reset the voicemaster configuration for your guild.
/voicemaster lock

Alias: vm lock

Lock your created personal voice channel so nobody joins unless permitted.
/voicemaster unlock

Alias: vm unlock

Reset the permissions on your personal voice channel so others can join.
/voicemaster hide

Alias: vm hide

Hide your voice channel so no users without administrative permissions can see it.
/voicemaster reveal

Alias: vm reveal

Reveal a previously hidden personal voice channel so others can see it.
/voicemaster kick

Alias: vm kick

kick a user out of your personal voice channel.
/voicemaster Permit

Alias: vm permit

Allow a user to join your voice channel if it is locked or hidden.
/voicemaster Claim

Alias: vm claim

Claim the voice channel created by another user if they have left that channel.
/voicemaster category

Alias: vm category

Set a category where personal voice channels will be created under.
/voicemaster region

Alias: vm region

Set the region for your voice channel.
/voicemaster bitrate

Alias: vm bitrate

Set a bitrate for your voice channels.
/voicemaster transfer

Alias: vm transfer

Transfer ownership of your personal voice channel to another user in the call.
/voicemaster rename

Alias: vm rename

Rename your personal voice channel.
/voicemaster limit

Alias: vm limit

Set a limit on how many people can join your personal voice channel.
text to speech
/speak

Alias: tts

Have wock join your voice channel and speak text
/speaklock

Alias: None

Lock text-to-speech to your account - everything you type in the channel will be spoken in voice for the next minute.Reset the voicemaster configuration for your guild.
/dialect

Alias: speech

Allows users to set their text to speech language and accent preferences.
/dialect reset

Alias: None

resets the TTS language and accent preferences to the default values
/preference

Alias: personality

Displays the TTS language and accent preferences of the mentioned user (or the command author).
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions, so we have answers.

Is wock free?

Streaming music with Wock is, and always will be, completely free. We believe in keeping public resources accessible without charging you for them.

Does Wock Work in All Servers?

Yes, Wock is built to function seamlessly in servers of any size, offering consistent performance and scalable features.

How Does Wock Handle Music Playback?

Wock streams music directly from platforms like YouTube, Spotify, and SoundCloud, ensuring high-quality playback without the need for downloads.

Is Wock Easy to Set Up?

Yes! Wock is designed to be simple to install and configure, so you can start using its features in minutes.

Can Wock Handle Large Communities?

Absolutely. Wock is optimized for performance and reliability, making it perfect for both small groups and large, active servers.

Will wock continue to gain commands?

Wock is consistently updated, with our development team actively incorporating new features and improvements to align with Discord's latest updates.
